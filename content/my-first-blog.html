My re-entry into philosophy

I was interested in philosophy a few years back in college. When I finished, however, I had too little time to get into it like I once was. But luckily, I have more free time than ever right now! I started by reading Think - Simon Blackburn, which was a great first step back. Currently finishing up Animal Farm - Geroge Orwell. My goal this time around is to get my feet wet with sections of philosophy I avoided when I was 17, like ethics and political philosophy.
